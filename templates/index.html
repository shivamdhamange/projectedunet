Analysis of 120 years of Olympic Events

The Olympic Games are the world's only truly global, multi-sport, celebratory athletics competition. With more than 200 countries participating in over 400 events across the Summer and Winter Games, the Olympics are where the world comes to compete, feel inspired, and be together.
This collaborative project, we embark on a dynamic exploration of 120 years of Olympic events. Our analysis spans various dimensions, including medals, countries, sports, age, and gender. Unveiling compelling narratives, we delve into the unique dynamics within different age and gender groups, scrutinize the distinct characteristics of sports categories, and examine what sets each country apart. Additionally, our interactive feature allows users to input any athlete's name, extracting intriguing details about their Olympic journey. Join us as we unfold the rich tapestry of Olympic history, unraveling stories that transcend time and celebrate the enduring spirit of athleticism.
